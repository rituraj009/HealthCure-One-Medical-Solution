Disease Detection Model

​In this project, we have developed a disease detection model that can identify seven diseases, allowing users to quickly determine their onset. These 7 diseases are Covid, Brain Tumor, Breast Cancer, Alzheimer, Diabetes, Pneumonia, heart Disease. The goal is to detect diseases quickly and accurately, without the need to wait for days for traditional test results. To achieve this, we've trained a deep learning model using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to extract features from the input images and predict the disease based on those features. This project has the potential to revolutionize disease detection by enabling people to get accurate results from the comfort of their own homes with just a few clicks.  This project is significant because it demonstrates the power of AI technology to do things that humans cannot. As more datasets become available, the accuracy of the model will improve, and the project can be expanded to detect more diseases in the future. By using cutting-edge techniques like CNNs, our project is poised to make a significant impact on the field of disease detection and diagnosis.
